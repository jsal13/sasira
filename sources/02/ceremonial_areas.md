# Ceremonial Areas

## Context

Inscribed on bronze plaques that were attached to what appears to be ceremonial altars throughout the temple complex. The plaques show signs of regular polishing and care, suggesting they were important to the inhabitants and actively maintained during regular ceremonies and rituals.

## Texts

### Text 1 - Main Ceremonial Altar Plaque
**Sasiran:** ზალ ყაᩴუ აზ ჯულ სომ უল ლასუ ᩴინ ალ ძურ იᩴუ ყის სირალ
**Notes:** Long, formal inscription spanning the entire bronze plaque

### Text 2 - Secondary Altar Inscription
**Sasiran:** აᩴუ ᩴარ ჯალიმ ურ იᩴუ სურ ან სარ
**Notes:** Shorter ceremonial text, possibly a standard blessing

### Text 3 - Ritual Preparation Area
**Sasiran:** ನური ჯალ ზურ ორ მირ სარ
**Notes:** Found on smaller altar used for preparation rituals

### Text 4 - Offering Bowl Rim
**Sasiran:** ძურუ ᩴალ საი აᩴუᩴ ᩴარუ სარ
**Notes:** Inscription around the rim of a ceremonial offering bowl
