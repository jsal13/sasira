# Sacred Archives & Inner Sanctum

## Context

Discovered in the deepest vault of the ancient library and hidden chambers behind the main altar. These texts were written in silver ink on vellum and sealed within crystal cases that required careful extraction. The chambers appear to have been used for the most sacred rituals, and the texts are interwoven with complex geometric patterns that may hold additional meaning. Surrounding documents suggest these may be prophecies or sacred decrees.
