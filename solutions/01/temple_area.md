# Temple Area Discoveries - Solutions

## Context
Found carved into the stone archway at the entrance to an ancient temple. The letters were filled with gold, suggesting this was an important inscription. Additional fragments were discovered on decorative stones in the temple's garden courtyard, where moss had grown over most inscriptions but some portions remained clearly visible.

## Text Translations

### Text 1 - Temple Entrance Archway
**Sasiran:** აშუ შალ სარ  
**Translation:** "God person is" / "The god is human"  
**Analysis:** Religious statement about divine nature, possibly indicating belief in divine incarnation

### Text 2 - Garden Courtyard Stone (Fragment A)
**Sasiran:** შინ ლარ  
**Translation:** "Sun great"  
**Analysis:** Fragment praising the sun, common in temple sun-worship contexts

### Text 3 - Garden Courtyard Stone (Fragment B)
**Sasiran:** სი შირ სარ  
**Translation:** "You beautiful are" / "You are beautiful"  
**Analysis:** Devotional address, possibly prayer to deity about beauty/divine nature

### Text 4 - Garden Path Marker
**Sasiran:** აש ზურ ან შის  
**Translation:** "Water love in priest"  
**Analysis:** Directional marker, possibly indicating sacred water location managed by priests

## Grammar Notes
- Basic present tense "სარ" (to be) constructions
- SOV word order maintained in complete sentences
- Fragments show typical noun-adjective patterns
- Religious/devotional vocabulary predominant
- Prepositions used for location references
